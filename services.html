    Our Services

Amazing Service

Tell us exactly what you're looking for, our Team are here to help you create your Ultimate Blue Cruise Holiday, from picking out the perfect vessel, choosing the best route & itinerary, to helping with bookings for day tours and activities. Every little detail is important to ensure your holiday is the best it can be!

Airport Transfers

For a smoother more enjoyable trip, we can arrange transfers to and from the airport so you don't have to worry! Just sit back relax and enjoy the journey, we'll take care of everything for you!

Blue Cruises

With so many beautiful places and hidden bays to visit along the Turquoise Coast, on a Blue Cruise we give you the oppourtunity to see more than one during your holiday! With destinations all around the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas we guarantee you'll fall in love the stunning scenery and natural beauty!

24 Hours a Day Support

Our teams are on standby 24 hours a day to help you with any enquiries or assistance that you may need throughout your stay!

Bed & Breakfast

Half Board

Our half board option includes breakfast and either lunch, or your evening meal fully prepared by our on board chef each day of your stay. A good choice for those who like to try a bit of the local cuisine.

Activities

Aboard each yacht, is a variety of activities and equipment that guests can use while on board. From snorkel and mask sets, fishing equipment and paddleboards to water sports such as Wakeboards and Jet Skiing. Most equipment is free to use on board, however fuel usage for the water sports will be charged accordingly.

Wakeboarding

Ride the Wake! A great water sport for those who love an adrenaline rush!

Snorkelling

Grab a snorkel and mask and take in the underwater scenery while floating on crystal clear waters.

Jet Ski

Take it as fast or as slow as you wish and ride out on a Jet Ski!

Ringo

Jump on the Ringo and hold on tight! This fun filled water sport will have you screaming with excitement!
