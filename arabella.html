    Arabella Gulet

The Arabella Gulet

An Ultra-Lux, Classic Gulet, and one of the most admired yachts to sail the Mediterranean and Aegean seas. Built in 2007 it was refitted in 2017, 2018, 2019 and most recently in Spring 2020. A total of 28 meters in length with a 7.10 meter beam. It boasts two 540 BHP engines and a top speed of 12 Knots. It has two 27kw & one 11 kw generators for providing electric all round, and a 4000 liter- 150 lt/hour fresh water tank . Originally registered under the American Flag, it was re-registered under the Turkish Flag in 2019.

Beautifully fitted with an extensive wood finish, Arabella is the true meaning of luxury. The cabins and living areas offer the space and comfort of larger yachts, all while still providing an intimate cruising experince for upto 8 people. 2 Master & VIP cabins and 2 twin single cabins, each with en-suite bathrooms. A large living area, bar and a fully equipped kitchen.

Salon Area

An indoor salon area with comfortable seating, a large dining table, large tv, and a bar. Provides access to the cabins and the kitchen.

The Master Cabin

The Master Cabin is decorated beautifully, with a fine wooden finish and carpeted for a soft touch under foot. A King-Size bed, TV with sattelite, wardrobe, make-up table, and a spacious en-suite bathroom with jacuzzi bath, separate shower and his & hers sinks.

The VIP Cabin

The VIP Cabin is also decorated with the same fine finish and carpeted floors. A Large double bed, wardrobes, TV with satellite, and an en-suite bathrom with shower & wc facilities.

Twin Cabin 1

Fitted with two single beds, our Twin cabins also have carpeted floors, a large satellite tv and en-suite bathroom & shower facilities.

Twin Cabin 2

Also fitted with two single beds, this cabin also has carpeted floors, a large satellite tv and en-suite bathroom & shower facilities.

The Sun Deck

The sun deck on the Arabella has room for 11 cushioned sunbeds and boasts a large sun shade which can be pulled out when required. There are also sets of masks and snorkels, fishing equipment and a range of water sports available for guests to use.

The Aft Deck

The aft deck boasts a large cushioned seating area ideal for relaxing in the evenings, alongside a large dining table, a fully equipped bar and a large hidden tv. The Aft deck also houses the helm of the boat.

The Kitchen

Our kitchen is fully equipped and prepared to serve you the most amazing food & drink during your stay on board.
